## Verdict

**Gate not met on any dataset.** NeuroDB's dot-product–softmax completion is beaten by a distance-based `KNNImputer` everywhere, and the gap *widens* as the data becomes more cleanly multimodal (better-separated clusters let KNN snap within-cluster while the softmax keeps blending). See `docs/ACCURACY.md` for the full characterization and the repositioning this implies.
